Wassim Mazouz

I am a graduate engineer from Centrale Lyon, specialized in Mathematics and Risk Engineering (MIR). In parallel, I completed the GRAF program in Quantitative Finance at ISFA.

Most recently, I was an AI & Quantitative Research Intern at BNP Paribas Global Markets – Data & AI Lab in Frankfurt, where I developed deep learning models for hedging with high-frequency financial data and collaborated with traders to adapt advanced research to market conditions.

Previously, I worked as a Machine Learning Research Intern at ENS Lyon within the OCKHAM team, contributing to benchmarking frameworks for optimization algorithms, and as a Student Researcher at LIRIS Laboratory, where I built generative models for image synthesis using VQ-VAE and deep learning techniques.

I am passionate about applying mathematics, machine learning, and quantitative research to complex challenges across finance, data science, and technology.
